		 <p class="featureFont">
		   The Oklahoma County Sheriff�s Foreclosure Sale is published monthly for two consecutive weeks at least thirty days prior to the date of sale.  The foreclosed properties are published in local newspapers in which the property is located.  The public may also buy property sale lists for $4.00 at the Oklahoma County Sheriff�s Office Civil Division located at 201 N. Shartel in Oklahoma City.
		 </p>
		 <p class="featureFont">
		   The Sheriff's Foreclosure Sales are located in the Oklahoma County Courthouse Annex Building at 320 Robert S. Kerr, Room 513, and 
		   start promptly at 2:00 p.m. every other Thursday.  
		 </p>
         <font class="featureFont">
		   To purchase a property you must meet the following requirements:
           <br /><br />   
         <li class="featureFont">Minimum bid must be at least two-thirds of the appraised price of the property.</li><br />
		   <li class="featureFont">Down payment of at least 10% of the purchase price is due within 24 hours of sale. The remaining 90% of the purchase price is due within 3 to 4 weeks of the sale date, before the confirmation hearing.</li>
	  </font>
		 <p class="featureFont">
		  Certified funds are required, made payable to Patricia Presley, Court Clerk. Any sale is subject to cancellation by the plaintiff.
		 </p>
		 <p class="featureFont">
		  Participants must provide photo identification in order to bid on properties.
		 </p>
		 <p class="featureFont">
		   For further information please contact Ann Marie Randolph at (405) 713-1056 or via e-mail at <a href="mailto:SOANNSEN@oklahomacounty.org?subject=Sheriff Sales">soannsen@oklahomacounty.org</a>
		 </p>
